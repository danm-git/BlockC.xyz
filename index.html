About Me

Hello! Most people call me Dan but I go by either Dan or Daniel! I am pretty laid back and I like to work hard, think hard and enjoy life with family and friends!

I was born in Boulder, Colorado and grew up just outside in a town called Broomfield. I attended Colorado State Univeristy where I met my future wife, Linnia.

After college, Linnia and I packed up or belongings and moved to Portland, OR! We lived in downtown Portland for 2 years when Linnia was accepted to Veterinarian school at Oregon State University! We packed up and moved to Salem, OR where she commuted to Corvallis for school and I commuted to Portland for work. After 2 years of Salem, a wedding, and 2 dogs, we packed up and moved to Corvallis, OR! Here, we made life-long friends and Linnia was able to fulfill her dream of becoming a Veterinarian!

After graduation we decided to spend a year living New York City and traveling the world! We found a very small apartment on the Upper West Side of Manhattan one block from Central Park. 1 year quickly became 5 with so many friends, travels and stories collected along the way! We then decided it was time to start a family. We moved to Huntington, Long Island where Linnia had been working as a Veterinarian. Our first son was born in New York City and we spent a year in Long Island before deciding to move back to Colorado!

We settled in a house North of Denver and now have 2 young and energetic boys! We have at least one permanent dog named Yeppi Queso and many foster dogs and cats come through our house as well.
