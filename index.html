About Me

Hello! Most people call me Dan but I go by either Dan or Daniel! I love hiking, traveling, running but mostly spending time with family and friends. I was born in Boulder, Colorado and grew up just outside in the town of Broomfield. I attended Colorado State Univeristy where I met my future wife, Linnia.

After college, Linnia and I packed up our belongings and moved to Portland, OR! We lived in downtown Portland for 2 years when Linnia was accepted to Veterinary school at Oregon State University! We then moved to Salem, OR where she commuted to Corvallis for school and I commuted to Portland for work. After 2 years of Salem, a wedding, and 2 dogs, we moved to Corvallis, OR! Here, we made life-long friends and Linnia was able to fulfill her dream of becoming a Veterinarian!

After graduation we decided to spend a year living in New York City and traveling the world! We found a very small apartment on the Upper West Side of Manhattan, one block from Central Park. 1 year quickly became 5 fun-filled years full of friends, food, travels and experiences collected along the way!

It was time to start a family so we moved to Huntington, Long Island where Linnia had been working as a Veterinarian. Our first son was born in New York City and after a year in Huntington we decided to move back to Colorado.

We settled in a house North of Denver and now have 2 young and energetic boys! We have one permanent dog named Yeppi Queso but many foster dogs and cats come through our doors.
